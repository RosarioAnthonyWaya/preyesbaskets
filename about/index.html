The Story Behind Our Gentle Gifting

A Boutique Gifting Studio With a Heart.

At Preye’s, we believe a gift should feel like a quiet moment of care — something soft, intentional, and unmistakably personal. Not rushed. Not predictable. Something chosen with heart.

What began as a simple love for thoughtful gifting has grown into a boutique studio where every box is curated with luxury, emotion, and a gentle attention to detail. We select items that feel comforting, elegant and lasting — the kind of pieces people keep, use, remember..

Our approach is simple:
slow curation,
beautiful presentation,
meaningful moments.
Every ribbon tied, every note handwritten, every scent and texture chosen with purpose.

Whether you’re celebrating, comforting, thanking or surprising someone, our gift boxes are designed to say the things words sometimes can’t — with warmth, grace and a touch of luxury.

This is gifting that feels personal.
This is gifting, the Preye’s way..
